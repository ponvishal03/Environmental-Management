Key Insights

  • Treatment Gap: Approximately 15-20% of daily waste remains untreated or sent to saturated dumpsites, though capacity is improving.

  • Legacy Challenges: The Deonar dumping ground remains a critical concern with ongoing efforts for biomining and capacity reduction.

  • Wet Waste Focus: BMC emphasizes decentralized rapid composting to reduce landfill load, mandating segregation at source.
