Strategic Priorities

Smart Monitoring & Data

  • Expand CAAQMS network
  • Integrated environmental data platform for analytics

Community Participation

  • Ward-level waste segregation campaigns
  • Citizen science programs for creek monitoring
Pilot Example "Clean Creek" citizen watch groups in Versova

Clean Mobility Strategy

  • Accelerated electrification of BEST bus fleet
  • Last-mile NMT (Non-Motorized Transport) connectivity
Pilot Example E-bus corridors & public EV charging hubs

Dust & Construction Control

  • Mandatory on-site enclosures & wheel washing
  • Real-time dust monitoring at major sites
Pilot Example Anti-smog guns deployment at metro sites

Circular Economy Models

  • Decentralized wet waste composting units
  • Material Recovery Facilities (MRFs) for plastics
Pilot Example Zero-waste ward initiatives in K-West

Blue-Green Infrastructure

  • Mangrove restoration & sponge city retrofits
  • Stormwater outfall gates with pumping stations
Pilot Example Miyawaki urban forests in open spaces
