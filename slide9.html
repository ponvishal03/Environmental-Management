Sources of Environmental Pollution

Sectoral Contributions & Emission Drivers

Pollution Analysis

Transportation & Mobility

Major contributor to NO2 and PM. Vehicular exhaust from aging fleets, congestion corridors, and road dust resuspension account for significant share of urban air pollution.

Construction & Demolition (C&D)

Rising infrastructure projects generate massive dust loads (PM10). Unregulated debris transport and lack of dust barriers are key issues.

Industrial & Power

Stack emissions from industrial zones (Mahul, Chembur), backup diesel generator sets, and localized solvent usage contribute to VOCs and SO2.

Waste & Sewage

Open burning of garbage, landfill fires (Deonar), and untreated sewage discharge into creeks/rivers affect both air and water quality.
