Conclusions & Strategic Priorities (2026–2030)

Roadmap for a Sustainable & Resilient Mumbai

Vision 2030

Health-First Air Strategy

  • Strict enforcement of construction dust mitigation norms (enclosures/sprinklers).
  • Accelerate transition to electric public transport (BEST) & curb DG set usage.
  • Implement Low Emission Zones (LEZ) in congested corridors.
Target KPI >85% Days meeting NAAQS limits

Fix Sewage-Stormwater Nexus

  • Eliminate illegal sewage outfalls into Mithi River & creeks; 100% diversion to STPs.
  • Upgrade STP capacity & treatment quality to tertiary standards for reuse.
  • Install trash booms at nullah mouths to prevent marine litter.
Target KPI 100% Sewage Treated (No dry-weather flow in drains)

Close MSW Treatment Gap

  • Scale decentralized wet-waste processing (biomethanation/composting) in wards.
  • Establish robust Construction & Demolition (C&D) waste recovery chain.
  • Complete scientific closure of Mulund & Deonar legacy dumps.
Target KPI >90% Waste Segregation at Source

Protect & Restore Green Assets

  • Strict Coastal Regulation Zone (CRZ) enforcement & removal of encroachments.
  • Mangrove restoration targets with satellite monitoring.
  • Develop urban forests/sponge parks for flood resilience.
Target KPI +500 ha Mangrove/Green Cover Restored

Data-Driven Governance & Transparency

  • Publish open, real-time dashboards for Air, Water, and Noise quality.
  • Conduct periodic scientific source apportionment studies (every 3 years).
  • Integrate early warning systems for air quality & flood risks.

Monitoring Infrastructure Targets:

Target KPI 1 Monitor per 1M Residents (Air & Noise)
Target KPI Real-time Public Data Availability
