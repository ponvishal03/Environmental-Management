Mithi River (18 km) Status

Critical pollution hotspot receiving raw sewage, industrial effluents, and solid waste. Flows through dense industrial/slum areas (Saki Naka, Dharavi) before entering Mahim Creek.

  • High BOD/COD Levels:

    Consistently exceeds safe limits; Dissolved Oxygen (DO) often near zero in lower stretches.

  • Solid Waste Dumping:

    Plastic and debris choke flow, exacerbating flood risks during monsoons.

Coastal & Creek Water Quality

11 Monitoring Stations (MPCB)
Variable Water Quality Index

Creeks (Mahim, Thane): High tidal influence but degraded by wastewater outfalls. WQI typically in 'Polluted' to 'Poor' range.
Coastal Sea: Better dilution; designated beaches monitored for recreational standards (Blue Flag pilot sites in state).

Monitoring Highlights

MPCB & BMC monitor 11 locations across Mumbai region. Data indicates urgent need for sewage treatment upgrades to prevent untreated discharge into creeks.
