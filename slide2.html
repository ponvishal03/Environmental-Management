Introduction: What is Environmental Quality (EQ)?

Foundations of Urban Sustainability & Health

Concepts & Context

Core Definition

"The condition of air, water, noise, land, and ecosystems relative to the requirements of human health and ecological integrity."

EQ Management Cycle

Continuous monitoring, regulation, mitigation, and public participation.

Why It Matters for Megacities

Direct impact on public health, economy, resilience, and quality of life.

Key Components

Air Quality, Water Security, Waste Management, Noise Control, Urban Green Cover.
