Key Challenges in EQ Management

Systemic Barriers & Risk Assessment

Challenges

Strategic Bottleneck

"Rapid urbanization outpaces infrastructure capacity, creating a compounding cycle of environmental stress and vulnerability."

Population Density & Sprawl

Unplanned expansion stresses basic services; high density (~20K/km²) amplifies exposure to pollutants and reduces per capita green space.

Flooding & Drainage

Aged stormwater drainage (SWD) system overwhelmed by high tides (Tidal Backflow) and climate-induced extreme rainfall events.

Enforcement & Compliance

Gaps in monitoring industrial discharge; challenges in regulating non-point sources like construction dust and vehicular emissions.

Informal Waste Sector

Lack of integration leads to unsafe recycling practices, open burning of waste, and difficulties in implementing segregation at source.

Data & Monitoring Gaps

Need for real-time, granular data on source apportionment to guide targeted policy interventions effectively.
