Co-Benefits & Services

  • Flood Defense: Acts as a natural sponge, absorbing tidal surges and excess monsoon runoff.
  • Carbon Sink: Sequesters carbon at 4x the rate of terrestrial forests (Blue Carbon).
  • Biodiversity: Nursery for fisheries; habitat for flamingos (Thane Creek Sanctuary).

Pressures & Threats

  • Infrastructure: Coastal Road, Bullet Train, and urban expansion impacting fringe areas.
  • Pollution: Plastic choking (non-biodegradable waste) and untreated sewage discharge.
