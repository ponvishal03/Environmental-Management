MPCB

State Regulator

Maharashtra Pollution Control Board

15 Air Stations 11 Water Stations

Operates CAAQMS network; conducts festival noise monitoring; industrial effluent testing labs.

Enforcement of:

• Air & Water Acts

• Hazardous Waste Rules

• Noise Pollution Rules

• Consent to Operate (CTO)

• Daily AQI Bulletins

• Legal Action / Closure Notices

BMC (MCGM)

Urban Local Body

Municipal Corp. of Greater Mumbai

Waste Tracking Local Air Sensors

Manages SWM facilities; monitors localized air quality; storm water drainage network.

Implementation of:

• SWM Rules 2016 (Segregation)

• C&D Waste Management Guidelines

• Mumbai Climate Action Plan

• Waste Collection Data

• Health Impact Surveillance

• Dust Mitigation Enforcement

CPCB

National Apex Body

Central Pollution Control Board

NCAP Portal NAMP Oversight

Standardizes monitoring protocols; oversees National Clean Air Programme (NCAP) funding.

Sets National Standards:

• NAAQS (2009) - 12 Parameters

• General Discharge Standards

• National AQI Dashboard

• Graded Response Action Plan (GRAP) Guidelines

Key Standards (NAAQS 2009)

PM10 (24h) 100 µg/m³
PM2.5 (24h) 60 µg/m³
NO2 (24h) 80 µg/m³

Public Data Platforms

• SAMEER App: Real-time AQI & Complaint filing

• MPCB Website: Daily bulletins & consent status

• SAFAR-Mumbai: Forecasts & advisories

Compliance Mechanism

"Polluter Pays" principle enforced through bank guarantees and environmental compensation charges. Regular stack monitoring and effluent sampling for industrial units.

Source: MPCB Annual Report 2023-24; CPCB Guidelines; BMC SWM Dept.
